A Hybrid Grey Wolf-Whale Optimization Algorithm for Optimizing SVM in Breast Cancer Diagnosis

In our research, a hybrid meta-heuristic swarm intelligence based Support Vector Machine Classifier called Grey Wolf-Whale Optimization Algorithm with Support Vector Machine (GWWOA-SVM) is proposed for early-stage detection of the disease. Anamalgamation of Whale Optimization Algorithm (WOA) and Grey Wolf Optimization (GWO) is used for tuning the hyper-parameters of SVM. The effectiveness of GWWOA-SVM is evaluated against the Wisconsin Breast Cancer Dataset (WDBC).

Indu Singh, Raghav Bansal, Anchal Singh, Ayush Gupta
